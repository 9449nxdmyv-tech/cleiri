title: "About Cleiri"
---

Cleiri, an award-winning singer and songwriter hailing from the vibrant Rio Grande Valley, is making waves in the music scene. Her unique sound combines the passion of Spanish ballads, the soothing sultriness of mariachi that pays homage to her roots in Jalisco.

With a career marked by success and creativity, Cleiri is currently crafting her debut studio album under the banner of CHR Records. Her music speaks volumes, reflecting her love, soul, and life experiences. Each note tells a story, inviting you to immerse yourself in her world.

Cleiri's previous work has already left a lasting impression. Her track "La Distancia" has found a home on syndicated Tejano radio stations across the nation. "Besos De Miel" and "Ilusiones," produced by Grammy-Award Winning Music Producer Alex Espinoza, known as ACE1, showcase her songwriting prowess and were featured in the Mexican film "Rooster."

In recognition of her talent and contribution to music, Cleiri was honored with an award from the prestigious Brownsville Music Legacy Awards in the fall of 2021.

Cleiri's latest masterpiece, "Estoy Pensando En Ti," produced by CHR Records CEO/Producer Rick Garcia is available now on all digital platforms! Get ready to embark on a musical journey that transcends borders and emotions.
